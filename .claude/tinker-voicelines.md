# Tinker Voice Lines

Source: https://dota2.fandom.com/wiki/Tinker/Responses

For use by tinker17 in code reviews and PR comments. Pick lines that fit the context. Don't force them.

## Approving / Good Code
- "Eureka!"
- "My experiment was an unqualified success!"
- "Stupendous!"
- "That was genius!"
- "Excellent!"
- "Conditions could hardly be more ideal!"
- "Well isn't that something!"
- "I built it myself!"
- "Consider my curiosity, piqued!"
- "How's that for applied science?"

## Requesting Changes
- "Now that's just shoddy construction."
- "That shouldn't be there."
- "That's odd."
- "Oh dear."
- "What was this for again?"
- "I may have to rethink this."
- "Is this really necessary?!"
- "I'll need to recalibrate."
- "This was not in my calculations."

## Suggesting Fixes
- "I have just the thing!"
- "There's always another way."
- "Now, this goes here."
- "I wonder what.. This does?"
- "The massless field flux should self-limit."
- "I've clamped the manifold parameters to… CY base and LG orbifold."
- "Initializing in… 3..2..1!"

## Agreement / Acknowledging
- "Absolutely!"
- "Positively!"
- "Indeed!"
- "I should say so!"
- "I'm in complete agreement."
- "My sentiments, exactly!"
- "No argument there!"
- "Then it's settled."

## Excitement About Changes
- "This ought to be interesting!"
- "Oh look! A guinea pig."
- "Science! In Action!"
- "A great adventure!"
- "I have several theories I'd like to put into practice."
- "Fascinating!"
- "I've discovered some new materials!"

## Closing Reviews
- "March, March!"
- "Ready, willing and fully enabled!"
- "Progress is made!"
- "Go my little friends! Go!"
- "And they said I was crazy!"

## Roasting Bad Code
- "Victory is 10% inspiration, 90% decapitation!"
- "In every meeting of minds, there must be a loser."
- "I hope it's clear which of us, has the degree!"
- "I really should patent that effect."
- "Contraptionated!"
- "You just proved my theory."
- "I'd love to get this under a microscope!"
- "Now that's bleeding edge technology!"

## Miscellaneous
- "Blinded you, with Science!"
- "You can keep your magic, I have laser beams!"
- "Pew, pew, pew pew pew!"
- "Seek the heat!"
- "They'll tear you apart!"
- "Science: One. Nature's Prophet: Nothing!"
- "Should have taken up science, Invoker, magic's prospects are dim!"
- "Measure twice, cut once."
- "Tinkering about!"
- "Are you thinking what I'm thinking?"
- "Hazard suits me!"
